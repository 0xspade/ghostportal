3. How We Use Your Data

4. Data Sharing

Your data is not shared with third parties except: SMTP provider (email delivery), and AI providers if AI generation is enabled (report content may be sent to the selected AI API). No advertising, no data sales, no unauthorized tracking.

5. Data Retention

  • Active reports: retained until manually deleted
  • Resolved reports: retained for 1 year after resolution
  • Access logs: retained for 2 years
  • Expired portal sessions: purged 90 days after expiry

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR and Philippine Republic Act 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correction / rectification
  • Erasure (where legally permitted — audit logs may be retained)
  • Data portability
  • Object to processing
  • Lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority

Philippine residents may lodge complaints with the National Privacy Commission at privacy.gov.ph.

7. Cookies & Storage

We use session cookies only: gp_session (owner) and gp_portal (security team). Both are HttpOnly, Secure, and set with appropriate SameSite policy. No third-party cookies, no advertising cookies, no analytics cookies.

8. Security Measures

Magic link (passwordless) authentication, CSRF protection, hCaptcha, AES-256-GCM backup encryption, TLS in transit, access logging, rate limiting, and server-side session management.

9. International Transfers

If AI providers are configured outside the operator's country, report content may be transferred to those providers' servers. The operator is responsible for ensuring this complies with applicable data protection law.

10. Children's Data

This platform is not intended for use by persons under 18. No data is knowingly collected from minors.
